About Me

Hey, I’m Harshita! I’m a first-year Computer Science Engineering student at IGDTUW, driven by the blend of logic and creativity in tech. I love tackling complex problems, crafting elegant solutions, and building seamless digital experiences. Whether it’s intuitive front-end design or optimizing back-end logic, I enjoy working across the stack, with a strong focus on UI/UX. Development, for me, is more than just code—it’s about creating experiences that resonate with users.

With a solid foundation in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) using C++, I thrive on writing efficient, scalable code. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me watching cricket, curating playlists, or conducting highly scientific experiments on how much coffee one person can consume before their keyboard starts blurring.

A perfect day? Cracking a challenging DSA problem and designing a UI that feels effortlessly intuitive. I’m still early in my journey, but that’s the best part—so much to learn, break, and rebuild better.
